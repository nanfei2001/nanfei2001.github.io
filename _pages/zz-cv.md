Profile
======
I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC Santa Barbara, advised by Prof. Mahnoosh Alizadeh. My work focuses on online learning, optimization, control, and market design.

My current research studies algorithms for decision-making under limited information, safety constraints, and incentive constraints, with applications in stochastic gradient methods, safe learning-based control, and EV charging systems.

Research Experience
======
**Strategic Querying for Stochastic Optimization**

UC Santa Barbara, September 2023-present

Study stochastic gradient methods with nonuniform and strategic query rules. The work focuses on query allocation, transient convergence, and the tradeoff between optimization performance and participant-side utility.

**Safe Online and Adaptive Control**

UC Santa Barbara, October 2023-present

Work on online nonstochastic control and safe learning-based LQR under state/input constraints. The goal is to obtain regret or performance guarantees while maintaining safety during learning.

**EV Charging Pricing and Market Design**

UC Santa Barbara, June 2024-present

Study pricing for EV charging systems with discrete station-access decisions and continuous charging decisions. This line uses convex and copositive duality to reason about pricing, allocation, and operator decisions.

**Machine Learning for Optimal Control**

UC Santa Cruz, May 2022-August 2022

Supervisor: Prof. Qi Gong

Worked on data-driven approximation methods for optimal control, including physics-informed neural network models for structured control functions.
