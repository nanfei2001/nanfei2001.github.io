About
======
The podcast is a Chinese-language conversational project about AI. It is not a business-analysis show, a tool tutorial, or an anxiety-driven news feed. Instead, it uses conversations with technically minded guests to think through how AI changes work, learning, knowledge, relationships, and the futures people can imagine.

The Next Elevator
======
The tentative name is 下一部电梯, or The Next Elevator. I like the image of a group of people waiting in front of an elevator whose doors have not opened yet. Everyone can hear it moving, everyone has a guess about what floor it is coming from, but nobody really knows who will be inside or where it will take them next. AI feels a bit like that: it is close enough to reshape daily life, but still strange enough that the usual language for work, intelligence, creativity, and relationships often feels insufficient. The podcast is meant to stay in that waiting space for a while, before the doors fully open, and ask what we are actually seeing.
