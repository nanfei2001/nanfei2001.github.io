Podcast
======
I am developing a Chinese-language podcast about AI, research, and the human questions that appear when technical systems enter ordinary life.

The tone I want is closer to a thought experiment and a conversation among fellow travelers than to AI news commentary. The show will aim to be technically serious, emotionally calm, and anti-anxiety: a place to talk about large questions without turning them into hype.

Possible Themes
======
  * How AI changes ordinary work, learning, and self-understanding.
  * What technical people see from inside research that is hard to explain in public discourse.
  * The gap between mathematical models, engineering systems, and human life.
  * Conversations with researchers, builders, and thoughtful non-specialists.

Status
======
The podcast is in early development. I am using this page as the future home for episodes, notes, transcripts, and related writing.
